Wei Peng | Stanford University
wepeng@stanford.edu

I am a Research Scientist at CNSlab, Stanford University, supervised by Prof. Kilian M. Pohl. I received my PhD from University of Oulu , Finland, where I was advised by Academy Professor Guoying Zhao. During my PhD study, I was lucky enough to have the opportunity to visit Harvard Medical School and CVL, ETH Zurich. Prior to that, I received the B.E. degree from UESTC, China, and Master degree from Xiamen University, China. My research interests include Machine Learning, Geometric neural networks, and Medical image analysis with a special emphasis on Computational Neuroscience. .
